Tikka

Not to be confused with Chicken Tikka Masala Tikka is a dish consisting of pieces of meat or vegetarian alternatives such as paneer with its origins tracing back to the Mughal dynasty. It is made by marinating the pieces in spices and yogurt and cooking them in a tandoor

Kala bhuna

Kala Bhuna is a famous dark and tender dish native to Chittagong prepared with shoulder pieces of beef and traditional spices; usually served during Mezban. It was originally served during the early 17th century. However, the origin of the dish is unknown.

Thai soup
